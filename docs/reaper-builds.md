# Reaper Builds & Strategy (P3R)

## About the Reaper

- Fought in Tartarus (appears on every floor after the first ~5 minutes)
- **No elemental weaknesses or resistances** — takes normal damage from everything
- **Immune to all ailments**
- **Vulnerable to critical hits** (takes extra crit damage)
- **2 actions per turn**
- Extremely high HP

### Skills Used
- All elements: Agidyne/Maragidyne, Bufudyne/Mabufudyne, Ziodyne/Maziodyne, Garudyne/Magarudyne
- Severe physical: Brave Blade, Vorpal Blade, Akasha Arts, Primal Force, Vile Assault
- Instant death: Hamaon/Mahamaon, Mudoon/Mamudoon
- Almighty: Megidolaon
- Buff/Debuff: Heat Riser, Debilitate, Dekaja
- Setup: Charge, Concentrate

## Reaper Strategy

### Turn 1 (Setup)
1. **Koromaru / Akihiko:** Debilitate on Reaper
2. **Aigis:** Marakukaja on party
3. **Switch to Trumpeter →** Charge
4. **Yukari:** Mediarahan or guard

### Turn 2 (Attack)
1. **Koromaru:** Re-Debilitate if needed (Reaper casts Heat Riser)
2. **Aigis:** Attack or Orgia Mode
3. **Switch to Masakado →** God's Hand (800 × 2.5 × 1.5 = **3000 effective power** after Trumpeter's Charge)
4. **Yukari:** Heal

### Ongoing
- **Keep Debilitate active** at all times — it cancels Reaper's Heat Riser
- **Guard** when Reaper uses Concentrate (Megidolaon incoming)
- **Bring Homunculi** for instant death protection (Mudoon/Mamudoon)
- **Crits** from Apt Pupil + Crit Rate Amp exploit Reaper's weakness
- **Masakado's physical immunity + Null Elec** covers all damage types
- Replace Null Elec with Repel Elec (Sword Q) if available for even better coverage

### Fallback: Orpheus Telos (lvl 91)
- If available, replace Masakado. Resists everything except Almighty (no weaknesses).
- 63 all stats, 8 inheritance slots, almighty inherit.
- Recipe: Thanatos + Asura + Chi You + Metatron + Helel + Messiah
